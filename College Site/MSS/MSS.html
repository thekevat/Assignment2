Measurement Systems and Sensors

The Measurement Systems and Sensors course at IET DAVV provides a fundamental understanding of modern instrumentation, measurement techniques, and sensor technologies. This course is essential for applications in automation, robotics, industrial control, biomedical engineering, and IoT.

  • Fundamentals of Measurement Systems – Characteristics of measurement systems, accuracy, precision, error analysis
  • Sensors & Transducers – Types of sensors (temperature, pressure, displacement, force, flow, etc.), working principles, selection criteria
  • Signal Conditioning & Processing – Amplification, filtering, analog-to-digital conversion (ADC), noise reduction
  • Temperature Measurement – Thermocouples, RTDs, thermistors, infrared sensors
  • Pressure & Flow Measurement – Manometers, piezoelectric sensors, anemometers, venturi meters
  • Displacement & Motion Sensors – LVDT, strain gauges, optical encoders, gyroscopes
  • Biomedical & Smart Sensors – ECG, EEG, biosensors, MEMS & IoT-based sensors
  • Data Acquisition Systems (DAQs) – Interface with microcontrollers and computers, industrial automation applications
