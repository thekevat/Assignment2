Numerical and Optimization Techniques

The Numerical and Optimization Techniques course at IET DAVV focuses on mathematical methods and computational algorithms to solve real-world engineering problems. The course equips students with skills in numerical analysis, optimization strategies, and algorithmic problem-solving, which are crucial in fields like scientific computing, data science, AI/ML, and engineering simulations.

  • Error Analysis – Types of errors, propagation, and precision
  • Root-Finding Algorithms – Bisection method, Newton-Raphson method, Secant method
  • Linear Algebraic Equations – Gauss elimination, LU decomposition, Iterative methods
  • Interpolation & Approximation – Lagrange interpolation, Newton’s divided difference
  • Numerical Differentiation & Integration – Trapezoidal rule, Simpson’s rule
  • Ordinary Differential Equations (ODEs) – Euler’s method, Runge-Kutta methods
