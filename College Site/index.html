The Institute of Engineering and Technology (IET), DAVV is a premier engineering institute affiliated with Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya (DAVV), Indore. Established in 1996, IET DAVV is known for its high academic standards, strong industry connections, and vibrant campus life.

Key Highlights:

IET DAVV offers B.Tech, M.Tech, and Ph.D. programs in various engineering disciplines, including Computer Science, Information Technology, Electronics & Telecommunication, Mechanical, Civil, and AI & Data Science.

Admissions to B.Tech programs are based on JEE Main, making it one of the most sought-after engineering colleges in Madhya Pradesh.

The institute encourages research and development, with students actively participating in hackathons, technical competitions, and industrial projects.

The college has a sprawling green campus, well-equipped labs, libraries, sports facilities, and an active student community.

IET DAVV has a strong placement record, with top recruiters like Microsoft, Amazon, Tata, Infosys, and Cognizant hiring students every year.
