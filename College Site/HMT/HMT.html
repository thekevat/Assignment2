Heat and Mass Transfer

The Heat and Mass Transfer course at IET DAVV explores the fundamental principles governing the transfer of heat and mass in engineering systems. This course is crucial for applications in thermal engineering, energy systems, HVAC, power plants, and industrial processes.

  • Fundamentals of Heat Transfer – Modes of heat transfer (conduction, convection, radiation), thermal conductivity
  • Conduction Heat Transfer – Fourier’s law, steady and unsteady heat conduction, heat transfer in composite walls and cylinders, thermal resistance network
  • Convection Heat Transfer – Natural and forced convection, boundary layer theory, heat transfer coefficients, applications in heat exchangers
  • Radiation Heat Transfer – Stefan-Boltzmann law, blackbody radiation, view factors, radiation shielding
  • Heat Exchangers – Types of heat exchangers (shell & tube, plate), LMTD and NTU methods
  • Boiling and Condensation – Film-wise and drop-wise condensation, boiling regimes, heat transfer correlations
  • Mass Transfer Basics – Diffusion and convective mass transfer, Fick’s law, mass transfer coefficients, applications in drying, distillation, and absorption
  • Numerical Methods in Heat Transfer – Finite difference methods, heat transfer simulations
