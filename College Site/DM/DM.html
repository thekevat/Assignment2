Dynamics of Machine

The Dynamics of Machines course at IET DAVV focuses on the study of forces, motion, and energy in mechanical systems. It provides students with a deep understanding of how machines operate under different conditions, helping them design and analyze mechanical systems for industries like automobile, robotics, aerospace, and manufacturing.

  • Fundamentals of Machine Dynamics – Kinematics of mechanisms, degrees of freedom, velocity & acceleration analysis
  • Force Analysis in Mechanisms – Static and dynamic force analysis of linkages, balancing of rotating and reciprocating masses
  • Gyroscopic Motion & Effects – Gyroscopic couple, stability in vehicles and ships
  • Cam & Follower Mechanisms – Types of cams, follower motion analysis
  • Gears & Gear Trains – Spur, helical, bevel gears, epicyclic gear trains
  • Flywheels & Governors – Energy storage in flywheels, centrifugal and inertia governors
  • Vibrations in Machines – Free and forced vibrations, damping, resonance, critical speeds of shafts
  • Brakes, Clutches, and Dynamometers – Analysis of braking force, friction clutches, power measurement
